What is "Promo Washing"? It's Literally Free Money.

You'll notice many sites offer welcome packages and promotional sales with discounted Sweeps Coins (SC). For example: pay $40 to receive $80 in SC. By purchasing these offers and "washing" them, you are capturing that discounted difference as pure profit.

If you want to make more money—upwards of $1,500+ per month on average—this is the method. It's really easy.

The 6-Step Washing Method

  1. Choose a high-value offer: As a rule, any offer where the SC value is at least 15-20% more than the purchase price is worth washing. All welcome bonuses listed below are highly profitable.
  2. Purchase the offer: You will receive the promotional SC in your account.
  3. Play through the SC (The "Wash"): Before redeeming, sites require you to play the promotional SC at least once. This is "washing".
  4. Use High-RTP Games: To minimize loss during the wash, you MUST play games with a high Return-to-Player (RTP). Blackjack with "Basic Strategy" is ~99.5% RTP and is the best tool. High-RTP slots are the next best option. The table below lists the best game for each site.
  5. Calculate Your Profit: In theory, you should keep around 95% of the SC you wash. For an offer where you pay $40 for $80 SC, keeping 95% leaves you with $76. That's a $36 profit for less than 30 minutes of effort.
  6. Rinse and Repeat: Consistently wash good offers as they appear across all sites. This is how you earn upwards of $1,500+ per month.
