What is Bonus Arbitrage?

Bonus arbitrage is a straightforward concept: companies offer cash rewards for new signups that are larger than the cost of trying their product or service.

The Core Strategy

  • Find high-value signup bonuses – Banks and fintech companies offer $50-$500 for opening accounts
  • Complete minimal requirements – Usually direct deposit or small deposits
  • Collect your bonus – Get paid through platforms that handle payouts

Why This Works

Customer acquisition costs are astronomical. Banks spend $200-$500 to acquire each new customer through traditional marketing. It's actually cheaper for them to pay you directly.

They're betting on retention. Most people forget to cancel. But if you're disciplined, you can take the bonus and walk away.

It's sustainable. Companies budget for this. As long as they're acquiring customers through advertising, they'll continue offering these bonuses.
